# Background Music Directory

Place your royalty-free background music files here.

## Supported Formats
- MP3
- WAV

## Music Sources
- YouTube Audio Library (https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary)
- Free Music Archive (https://freemusicarchive.org)
- Incompetech (https://incompetech.com)
- Bensound (https://www.bensound.com)

## Important
Make sure you have the rights to use any music in your videos.
Only use royalty-free or properly licensed music to avoid copyright strikes.

## Volume
The system automatically adjusts background music to 20% volume to not overpower the narration.
